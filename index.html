About me

My name is Ali Hassan Shah. I am a Senior Embedded Software Engineer currently working at AMD in Shanghai, China, where I develop RAS (Reliability, Availability, Serviceability) tools for MI-Series GPUs including MI300X and MI308 models. My expertise spans C/C++, Python, embedded AI, hardware design, TDD practices, and system-level software development.
